People Detection and Counting

AI-powered systems can use computer vision to detect and count people in real-time from video feeds, aiding in crowd management, store analytics, and public safety by analyzing patterns of movement and density.

Speech Recognition Systems

AI speech recognition converts spoken language into text, enabling voice-controlled applications, transcription services, and accessibility features for users with disabilities, enhancing user experience and efficiency.

Dynamic Pricing And Strategy

AI algorithms analyze real-time market trends, customer behavior, and competitor pricing to adjust prices dynamically, optimizing revenue and maintaining competitiveness in industries like e-commerce and travel..
